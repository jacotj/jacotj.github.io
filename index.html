    <section id="research" class="py-5">

      <div class="container">

        <h2 class="section-title">Research</h2>

        <div class="row g-4">

          <div class="col-lg-4">

            <article class="research-card overflow-hidden">

              <img src="assets/images/research-reconstruction.jpg" alt="Illustration of laboratory-grown heart tissue for surgical reconstruction" class="research-img">

              <div class="p-4">

                <h3 class="h5">Laboratory-Grown Heart Tissue for Surgical Reconstruction of Structural Defects in Newborns</h3>

                <p class="mb-0">Our goal is to isolate stem cells from amniotic fluid before birth and create heart tissue that can be used to reconstruct the heart with the baby’s own cells after birth.</p>

              </div>

            </article>

          </div>

          <div class="col-lg-4">

            <article class="research-card overflow-hidden">

              <img src="assets/images/research-development.jpg" alt="Illustration of mechanics of heart development" class="research-img">

              <div class="p-4">

                <h3 class="h5">Mechanics of Heart Development</h3>

                <p class="mb-0">We use laboratory-grown heart tissue to understand proliferation, migration, and force generation in heart structure formation and how these are altered in genetic disease.</p>

              </div>

            </article>

          </div>

          <div class="col-lg-4">

            <article class="research-card overflow-hidden">

              <img src="assets/images/research-manufacturing.jpg" alt="Illustration of biomanufacturing of heart tissue" class="research-img">

              <div class="p-4">

                <h3 class="h5">Biomanufacturing of Heart Tissue</h3>

                <p class="mb-0">We investigate scaffold composition, manufacturing methods, bioreactors, and controlled release strategies to improve consistency, cost, and translation.</p>

              </div>

            </article>

          </div>

        </div>
